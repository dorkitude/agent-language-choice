## Summary

### What changed

**`src/dndPlugin.ts`** вЂ” three additions:

2. **Auth section** (in-memory `users` map + helpers):

   - `registerUser` вЂ” validates `username` (`/^[a-z0-9_-]{2,32}$/`), `password` (в‰Ґ8 chars), `role` (`dm`|`player`); returns `409` on duplicate; responds `{username, role}`.
   - `loginUser` вЂ” malformed (non-string fields) в†’ `400`; no match / wrong password в†’ `401`; success в†’ `{username, token: "session-<username>"}` (deterministic token).

3. **Routes** wired into the POST router: `POST /v1/auth/register` and `POST /v1/auth/login`.
